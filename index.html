When you come play the Jay and Lionel Municipal Golf Course you will enjoy some of the best golf in Acadiana. Our gorgeous golf course is a favorite among local golfers and regularly plays host to golf outings and other charitable events. Just east of Highway 90 and south of Interstate 10, we are easy to find and easy to play. Our idyllic settings will let you step away from life for a little while and focus on your golf passion. Our professionally engineered course will challenge you to lower your score on our fairways, numerous water hazards and carefully placed sand traps. Immediately after your round of golf, you will be eager to return and play more.

Having been in the golf business for a long time, we have the best golf membership and lesson packages to suit you. We encourage you to request a tour of our facility, and welcome any questions you may have. Contact our Pro Shop for all the details and benefits to our golf lessons and memberships.
